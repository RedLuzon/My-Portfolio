How to Play

Master the game in three simple steps

Step 01

Set the Range

Choose your difficulty by setting the maximum number limit. The higher the number, the harder the challenge.

Step 02

Make Your Guess

You have 3 attempts to guess the correct number. Enter your prediction and submit to see if you match the computer's choice.

Step 03

Win or Retry

Guess correctly to trigger the victory celebration! Run out of attempts? The number is revealed and you can try again immediately.

Pro Tip

Use the hints! After each wrong guess, the game tells you if your number was too high or too low. Use this information to narrow down your next attempt strategically.
